Forecasting and prediction

I have worked on predictive modelling across industries on problems with limited data resources (crash prediction from public data), rare outcomes (website visitor conversion) and uncertain targets (marketing budget forecasting). My experience gives me an edge when it comes to building predictive data products.

Policy analysis

I obtained my PhD working at the RAND Corporation on projects related to health, infrastructure and public policy. I am formally trained in econometrics and have applied these methods to assess health programs and risk.

Text analytics

I have extensive experience leveraging text data in analytics products for academic research, marketing and risk assessment. I have also given talks and taught courses on the subject.

Training/Mentoring

I find sharing my experience with others to be extremely rewarding. I have constructed and taught courses and workshops throughout my career and provided mentorship to coworkers and the community.

Building Data Science capacity

I have built three data science teams from the ground up and have worked as a consultant helping companies build and scope their data science practice. These groups continue to deliver impact and expand on the structure that we founded.

Community contributions

I maintain a close engagement with the tech community by organizing meetups, contributing to social good projects and regularly posting projects and interesting material to my blog.
